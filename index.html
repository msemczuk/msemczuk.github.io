About Me

Hello! I am an astrophysicist working on the dynamics and morphology of galaxies. I completed my PhD at the Nicolaus Copernicus Astronomical Center in Warsaw in 2019. After that, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Leicester for four years before moving to the Gaia Group at the Universitat de Barcelona in 2023.

In my work, I study the formation, evolution, and dynamics of morphological features in spiral galaxies. These include bars (both single and double), spiral arms, and warps. I often use computer simulations of galaxies, which I compare with observational data of the Milky Way and external galaxies.
